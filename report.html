A quick snapshot

Where things moved this month. Short version, not the full deck.

The shift

Afrofit moved from one-off posts to one connected campaign, "This Is The Place," running across billboards, social, a member newsletter and, for the first time, paid ads.

What's now live or rolling out

  • Two cinematic billboards (Feel It Again, This Is The Place) with real faces from every club.
  • The July newsletter, a tailored version for Delta, TRM and Kisumu.
  • A 12-reel and 6-carousel series in production, released in three monthly chapters.
  • Paid ads live on Meta, sending leads straight to WhatsApp at each club.

Carousels & reels in the new series

The numbers point the same way every time: real people and real authority win. The best post in Afrofit's history is a tribe-member carousel; the strongest recent reels are the educational ones like "Coordination is something you earn." Across the account, reels, carousels and images all pull similar engagement, so the campaign deliberately uses all three formats. Every piece this month is built on exactly that, community and expertise, tied back to the club.

New channels this month

Paid ads and a direct member newsletter, two routes Afrofit wasn't using before. The ads only went live this week, so the read on them comes next month with real cost-per-lead numbers.
